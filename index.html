About

The Somerville Metro Lions Club was conceived in the spring of 2007 when then-District 16D Vice Governor Tony Aba√±o and Larry Massaro, who served as guiding Lions, met with Ray Tigol, Rogel Bautista, Jack Domider and Prospero Tayco and discussed the formation of a Lions Club in Somerville, NJ. The club was formed with 26 members, with Ray Tigol becoming the charter president. As a new Club, SMLC started being one of the first clubs of District 16D with family membership, i.e., husbands and wives joining together.
SMLC celebrated its Charter Night & Induction last January 26, 2008 at the Bridgewater Manor located in Route 202/206 Bridgewater, New Jersey. The event was attended by officers of Mulitple District 16 of New Jersey Lions and guests from the local government. As part of this historic affair, the Club published a souvenir journal.

Donating to RWJUH!

On April 15th, the Somerville Metro Lions Club presented a total donation of $2600 to the RWJUH Somerset Health Care Relief Foundation for the purpose of purchasing personal protective equipment (PPE) for the staff. Even during this global pandemic, WE SERVE.

Bread for the Homeless!

Approved as a recurring club project, members of the Somerville Metro Lions Club collected bread from a local bakery in Watchung, NJ and delivered them to a social hall in Elizabeth, NJ, to be distributed to the homeless and less fortunate members of the community.

Projects

Eyeglasses Recycling

Eyeglasses.com is a collection site for the Lions Club International Eyeglass Recycling program. A pair of eyeglasses that are no longer being used can change another person's life.

Somerset County Food Banks

The Food Bank Network distributes food and to provide other basic human needs to those less fortunate in our community in a manner which recognizes and advances self worth and human dignity.

Feeding Hands at Emmanuel Church

Emmanuel Church has partnered with Feeding Hands, a Christian, private, non-profit organization that provides almost 10,000 pounds of meat, produce and non-perishables each month to those who need it.
